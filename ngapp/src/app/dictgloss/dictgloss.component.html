How to use

1. Enter an Irish passage into the first text input. 
2. Listen to the voice synthesis that is given from the passage. 
3. Try to rewrite it by ear (you can see the first letter of each word by clicking its space). 
Tip: If you're stuck, you can click on one of the spaces where the words go and get the first letter of it (this will help as it's case sensitive).
